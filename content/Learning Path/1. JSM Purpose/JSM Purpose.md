The JSM is a framework of systems for living effectively. It provides support in reaching long-term goals efficiently while staying happy, healthy and sociable.

The knowledge content of the JSM avoids giving specific examples where possible. The purpose of this content is to educate you to create your own goals, habits and systems, not to blindly follow what is recommended to you.

The JSM's collection of resources is constantly evolving, yet it will never be fully complete. Chasing perfection is futile, but heuristics created along the way can end up being truly valuable.
