///// STEPS /////

1. List priorities and optionals
2. Define final cutoff time for work
3. Begin short time-boxes with breaks
4. Stay aligned

///// CONCEPT /////

To get a lot of things done in the key hours of the day, first understand what those things are, then repeat short time boxes of them with breaks, and have a final cutoff time in mind to encourage action.
