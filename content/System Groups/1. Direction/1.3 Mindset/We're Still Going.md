Sometimes, all that is needed is a shift in perspective. We move through life, but we forget what that means.

When waking up in the morning, you can make a habit of remembering not everyone gets to do that. To live another day is a luxury. If your life is going generally well, and everyone around you wakes up safely too, this feeling should be even stronger.

Comparing your life to a roguelike video game is a fun experiment for this. Some example thoughts from this mindset are "damn, we could take this run to the moon if we play it right" and "how is this run still going? I got blessed". And just like a roguelike, making the most of what you've been randomly given is the best way forward.
