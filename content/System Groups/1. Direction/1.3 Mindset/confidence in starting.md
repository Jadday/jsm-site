Why am I scared to start?

And once I start, why am I scared to keep going?

It's a good question, but does it even need an answer? The solution to fear is blind confidence. Forget emotions, tiredness, commitments, issues from the past, worries, regrets, all of it. Go in with blind confidence now and it will just work.

Don't be scared to start. Do it as soon as possible so you don't have to go through the struggles of it again later. And don't be scared to keep going. Be strong, committed, do too much on purpose so you can reel it back in later. Test things, even if they're not perfect long term systems, don't just take issues as a reason to stop.
