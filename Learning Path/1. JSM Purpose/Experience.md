Experience in a certain activity or field comes from investing lots of time and energy into it, and exploring many different situations it presents. It is different to skill, which can be gained in a short time with the right techniques. Experience is more about understanding and involvement.

Everyone has experience in their lives, but where it is placed varies wildly. Some have lots of social experience. Some have lots of experience with productivity. There is no superior area to have it. But the JSM only exists and iterates because I continue to gain more experience with the concepts of success, productivity and more.

Experience can also exist at any layer. Some may know their goals clearly after lots of time thinking about them. Some might have enough experience to know exactly which habits they want in their life, and have reliable systems for sustaining them. Some may have lots of experience dealing with the chaos of the world, making them resistant to sudden changes and stress.
