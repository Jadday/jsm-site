///// STEPS /////

1. Open big pack, split single portions into small containers
2. Label with date of consumption
3. Store out of immediate sight

///// CONDITION /////

Upon getting home from a shopping trip where treat items were purchased.

///// PURPOSE /////

After purchasing snacks, this system exists to ensure that they are eaten over time, in reasonable portions. This shifts the habit from eating one portion (reactive mental battle) to separating portions out (proactive).
