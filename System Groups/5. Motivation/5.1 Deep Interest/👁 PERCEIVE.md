To use deep interest as a tool, you just have to find a creative way to look deeper into the subject matter, and get into the learning mindset.

Examples:
- For AWS, deep interest is actually starting a project there. Making some basic product, and using AWS to put it out to the web. 
- For art, deep interest is not just mindlessly practicing. When you're drawing something, it's perceiving that thing and looking deeper. Then asking questions. How do I do the colours for this? How do I draw that texture? How do I do the shadow? And so on. Keep going deeper.
